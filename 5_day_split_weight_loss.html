This is your workout based on your preferences

To lose weight, means that you need to burn more calories that you intake. A great way to do this is to walk/run a lot. Before every weightlifting workout do at least 15 minutes of treadmill or bicycle machine. You can use your prefered settings, but here is a recommended treadmill exercise: 15 minutes on 5.6 km/h on 15 incline

Now lets talk about your weightlifting workout. Your split is going to be in 5 days per week, so each day you will be hitting different muscle groups. Here is each day:

Day 1: Chest and Triceps

This day focuses your chest and triceps. Some exercises consist of:

Bench Press: 3 sets of 10 reps
Chest Flyes: 3 sets of 12 reps
Tricep Dips: 3 sets of 10 reps
Rope tricep pulldown: 3 sets of 12 reps
Push-ups: 3 sets of 10 reps

Day 2: Back and Biceps

This day focuses your back and biceps. Some exercises consist of:

Lat Pulldowns: 3 sets of 10 reps
Bent-Over Rows: 3 sets of 12 reps
Cable Rows: 3 sets of 10 reps
Bicep Curls: 3 sets of 12 reps
Hammer Curls: 3 sets of 10 reps

Day 3: Legs

This day focuses your legs only. Some exercises consist of:

Smith machine squat: 3 sets of 10 reps
Lunges: 3 sets of 12 reps
Leg Press: 3 sets of 10 reps
Leg Extensions: 3 sets of 12 reps
Leg curl:3 sets of 12 reps

Day 4: Shoulders and Abs

This day focuses on your shoulders and the abdominal muscle group. Some exercise consist of:

Shoulder press: 3 sets of 10 reps
Lateral raises: 3 sets of 10 reps
Face pull: 3 sets of 10 reps
