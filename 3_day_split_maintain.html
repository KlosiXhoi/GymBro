This is your workout based on your preferences

To maintain your weight means, to have a balance between the calories you burn and the calories you intake. If you want to mainatain your currnt weight, you are going to lose fat and gain a lot of strength.

For your 3 day split we recommend the Push Pull Legs workout.

Day 1: Push Day

Push day focuses your chest, triceps and the front part of your shoulders. Some exercises consist of:

Bench Press: 3 sets of 10 reps
Incline dumbbell bench press: 4 sets of 10 reps
Chest flyes:3 sets of 12 reps
Rope tricep pulldown:3 sets of 10 reps
Skullcrushers:3 sets of 10 reps
Lateral raises:3 sets of 12 reps

Day 2: Pull Day

Pull day focuses your back, biceps and the rear part of your shoulders. Some exercise consist of:

Lat pulldown: 4 sets of 10 reps:
Cable row: 4 sets of 10 reps
Rope pullover: 3 sets of 10 reps
Deadlift: 4 sets of 10 reps
Bicep curl: 3 sets of 10 reps
Hammer curl: 3 sets of 12 reps
Face pull: 3 sets of 10 reps

Each exerccise should be performed in 3 to 4 sets, whith each set having 10 to 15 reps.

Day 3: Leg Day

Leg day focuses on different part of your legs, such as quads, hamstrings, glutes. On this day you can also do exercises for the middle part of your shoulders. Some exercises consist of:

Smith machine squat: 4 sets of 12 reps
Leg press: 4 sets of 12 reps
Leg extension: 3 sets of 12 reps
Leg curl: 3 sets of 12 reps
Lunges: 3 sets of 12 reps
Shoulder press: 3 sets of 12 reps

AFTER EACH WORKOUT SESSION YOU SHOULD DO 10 TO 15 MINUTES OF CARDIO. WE RECOMMEND A TREADMILL ON THIS SETTINGS: 15 minutes on 5.6 km/h on 15 incline.
