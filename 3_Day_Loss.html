This is your workout based on your preferences

To lose weight, means that you need to burn more calories that you intake. A great way to do this is to walk/run a lot. Before every weightlifting workout do at least 15 minutes of treadmill or bicycle machine. You can use your prefered settings, but here is a recommended treadmill exercise: 15 minutes on 5.6 km/h on 15 incline

Now lets talk about your weightlifting workout. Since its 3 days a week, you are going to do a Push, Pull, Legs workout (PPL).

Day 1: Push Day

Push day focuses your chest, triceps and the front part of your shoulders. Some exercises consist of:

Bench Press
Incline dumbbell bench press
Chest flyes:
Rope tricep pulldown:
Sullcrushers:
lateral raises:

Each exerccise should be performed in 3 to 4 sets, whith each set having 10 to 15 reps.

Day 2: Pull Day

Pull day focuses your back, biceps and the rear part of your shoulders. Some exercise consist of:

Lat pulldown:
Cable row
Rope pullover
Bicep curl
Hammer curl
Face pull

Each exerccise should be performed in 3 to 4 sets, whith each set having 10 to 15 reps.

Day 3: Leg Day Leg day focuses on different part of your legs, such as quads, hamstrings, glutes. On this day you can also do exercises for the middle part of your shoulders. Some exercises consist of:

Smith machine squat
Leg press
Leg extension
Leg curl
Shoulder press

Each exerccise should be performed in 3 to 4 sets, whith each set having 10 to 15 reps.
