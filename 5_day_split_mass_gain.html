This is your workout based on your preferences

To gain mass, means to build more muscles and earn more fat on your body. Usually this is done to increase body weight and look bigger.

For your 5 day split we recommend this workout

Day 1: Chest and Triceps

Bench Press: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
Dumbbell Bench Press: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
Chest Flyes: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
Skullcrushers: 3 sets of 10-12 reps

Day 2: Back and Biceps

Lat Pulldown: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
Cable Row: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
Bent Over Row: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
Preacher Curl: 3 sets of 10-12 reps

Day 3: Legs and Shoulders

Squat: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
Romanian Deadlift: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
Shoulder Press: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
Lateral Raises: 3 sets of 10-12 reps

Day 4: Chest and Triceps

Incline Bench Press: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
Incline Dumbbell Bench Press: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
Chest Flyes: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
Tricep Extension: 3 sets of 10-12 reps

Day 5: Legs and Biceps

Deadlift: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
Sumo Deadlift: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
Bulgarian Split Squat: 3 sets of 10-12 reps per leg
Bicep Curl: 3 sets of 10-12 reps

Perform each exercise with proper form and technique, focusing on muscle engagement.

Rest for about 1-2 minutes between sets.

Adjust the weights according to your fitness level and aim to increase the weight gradually as you progress.

Warm up before each workout and cool down/stretch afterward to prevent injuries.
